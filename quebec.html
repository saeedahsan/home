Quebec

The Province of Quebec was founded on July 1st, 1867 when the Dominion of Canada was made. Quebec was preceded by the colonies of Canada East, (British) a division of the former Province of Canada, Upper Canada, (British) Quebec, (British) and New France (French) . It was one of the first four provinces along with Ontario, Nova Scotia, and New Brunswick. It was named after the former British colony of Quebec which was located around where modern-day Quebec is.

Fast Facts

  • Capital City: Quebec City
  • Motto: "Je me souviens" (I remember)
  • Area: 1.667 million km2
  • Population: 8.215 million
