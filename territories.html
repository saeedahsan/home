The Territories

Canada has three territories which are located North of the provinces. The territories are: Northwest Territories, Yukon, and Nunavut. Territories in Canada have less political power when compared to the provinces. Nunavut is the only territory that has its own motto.

Fast Facts

  • Created On: Northwest Territories- July 15th, 1870 Yukon- June 13th, 1898 Nunavut- April 1st, 1999
  • Capital City: Northwest Territories- Yellowknife Yukon- Whitehorse Nunavut- Iqaluit
  • Motto: "Nunavut Sannginivut" (Our land, our strength)
  • Area: Northwest Territories- 1.346 million km2 Yukon- 482,443 km2 Nunavut- 2.093 million km2
  • Population: Northwest Territories- 41,462 Yukon- 35,874 Nunavut- 35,944
