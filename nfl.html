Newfoundland and Labrador

Newfoundland and Labrador was created on March 31st, 1949. It was the last remaining significant British colony in North America. The only other provinces that changed directly from a colony to a province are Ontario, Quebec, Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, and British Columbia. The Newfoundlanders had a choice between Canada and the United States, and they voted on Canada.

Fast Facts

  • Capital City: St.John’s
  • Motto: "Quaerite Prime Regnum Dei" (Seek ye first the kingdom of God)
  • Area: 405,212 km2
  • Population: 528,448
