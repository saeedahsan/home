Saskatchewan

Sakatchewan is a praire provine which joined Canada on September 1st, 1905. Saskatchewan’s "inaugragation day" was made on September 4th. It, like alot of praire provinces, had broken up from the Northwest Territories.

Fast Facts

  • Capital City: Regina
  • Motto: "Multis E Gentibus Vires" (From many people’s strength)
  • Area: 651,900 km2
  • Population: 1.13 million
