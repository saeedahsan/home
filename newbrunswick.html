New Brunswick

The Province of New Brunswick was founded on July 1st, 1867 when the Dominion of Canada was made. New Brunswick was preceded by the colonies Of New Brunswick (British) and Acadia (French). It was one of the first four provinces along with Ontario, Quebec, and Nova Scotia. It was named after the former British colony of New Brunswick which was located around where modern-day New Brunswick is.

Fast Facts

  • Capital City: Fredericton
  • Motto: "Spem Reduxit" (Hope restored)
  • Area: 72,908km2
  • Population: 753,914
