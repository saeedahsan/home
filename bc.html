British Columbia

British Columbia was a British colony that joined Confederation on July 20th, 1871. It is the westernmost province of Canada, with a Pacific coast. British Columbia's capital, Victoria, is located on Vancouver Island.

Fast Facts

  • Capital City: Victoria
  • Motto: "Splendor Sine Occasu" (Splendour without diminishment)
  • Area: 944,735 km2
  • Population: 4.631 million
