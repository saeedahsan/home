Ontario

The Province of Ontario was founded on July 1st, 1867 when the Dominion of Canada was made. Ontario was preceded by the colonies of Canada West, (British) a division of the former Province of Canada, Lower Canada, (British) Quebec, (British) and New France (French) . It was one of the first four provinces along with Quebec, Nova Scotia, and New Brunswick.

Fast Facts

  • Capital City: Toronto
  • Motto: "Ut Incepit Fidelis Sic Permanet" (Loyal she began, loyal she remains)
  • Area: 1.076 million km2
  • Population: 13.6 million
