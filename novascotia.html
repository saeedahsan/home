Nova Scotia

The Province of Nova Scotia was founded on July 1st, 1867 when the Dominion of Canada was made Nova Scotia was preceded by the colonies Of Nova Scotia (British) and Acadia (French). It was one of the first four provinces along with Ontario, Quebec, and New Brunswick. It was named after the former British colony of Nova Scotia which was located around where modern-day Nova Scotia is.

Fast Facts

  • Capital City: Halifax
  • Motto: "Munit Haec Et Altera Vincit" (One defends and the other conquers)
  • Area: 55,283km2
  • Population: 942,926
