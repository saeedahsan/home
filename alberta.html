Alberta

Alberta was also founded on September 1st, 1905. It is located just west of Saskatchewan and was also created from the original Northwest Territories.

Fast Facts

  • Capital City: Edmonton
  • Motto: "Fortis et Liber" (Strong and free)
  • Area: 661,848 km2
  • Population: 4.146 million
