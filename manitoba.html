Manitoba

Manitoba was created July 15th, 1870 as a division of the Northwest Territories. They were made after a request from the Metis. The Metis were descendants of both aboriginal and French parents. The province started off very small but the people got angry so the government made it bigger. Once it grew to its modern size, the capital was moved to Winnipeg.

Fast Facts

  • Capital City: Winnipeg
  • Motto: "Gloriosus et Liber" (Glorious and free)
  • Area: 647,797 km2
  • Population: 1.282 million
