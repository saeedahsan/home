Prince Edward Island

Prince Edward Island is the smallest province in Canada. It officially joined Canada on July 1st, 1873. The island used to be called Saint John’s Island when it was a colony. Once it joined Confederation, the name was changed to Prince Edward Island.

Fast Facts

  • Capital City: Charlottetown
  • Motto: "Parva Sub Ingenti" (The small under the protection of the great)
  • Area: 5,660 km2
  • Population: 146,283
